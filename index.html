Discover Your Solutions

Like a river finding its natural course, we'll help you navigate towards your authentic path

Did you ever contemplate a river and its running water? Or a pond and its still water?

If the pond seems to be inhabited by dark life and the river by a vivid stream, the magic about these two phenomenons is that each of them has a specific function and together they contribute to the natural stability of aquatic life.

On the basis of this ecosystem example, gbcoaching proposes a process of exploration to open new channels. Instead of struggling against what happens, we shall move forward with the movement, just like a surfer making the best of the wave.

This process will bring you benefits such as improvement in terms of:

  • Motivation and self-esteem
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Self-leadership abilities
  • Positive emotional management
  • Quality of life and authenticity

Your Coaching Journey

A clear, structured approach to help you transition from awareness to action

1
Introductory Session
Establish initial contact and confirm the coaching process meets your needs and expectations.
2
Goal Setting
Define clear objectives and create a personalized plan adapted to your lifestyle and preferences.
3
Active Coaching
Engage in 4-8 sessions (90 minutes each) or flexible scheduling options including phone sessions.
4
Transformation
Develop your personal strategy and action plan to reach your goals authentically and successfully.

Coaching is based on partnership and personal engagement within the International Coach Federation's (ICF) Code of Ethics. All personal data is kept strictly confidential.

About Gianna Battolo

Professional coach certified by the Institut de Coaching (IDC) in Geneva

Certifications:

  • • Institut de Coaching (IDC), Geneva
  • • Associate Certified Coach (ACC), ICF
  • • Business Administration (HEC)
  • • Management and Analysis of Public Policy (DESMAP)

My name is Gianna Battolo, and I'm a professional coach certified by the Institut de Coaching (IDC) in Geneva, and Associate Certified Coach (ACC) within the International Coach Federation (ICF), where I am an active member of the ICF Switzerland Committee.

Working for more than 20 years at national and multinational companies has provided me the opportunity to understand and absorb various types of organisational and managerial cultures, as well as the value of an organization's human resources.

Due to periods of economic downturn and financial crisis over the years, I had many opportunities to observe how multinational companies and their staff evolve through significant change such as innovation, mergers or reorganization.

In 2007, my sights turned in a new direction. After IDC training, I decided to create gbcoaching with the aim of supporting individuals and/or groups during phases of professional transition, conflict resolution or simply the need for space to spread their wings!

"When the heart is calm, everything becomes clear."

I feel a real passion for movement and that's why I practice taichi where the tradition of this martial art teaches us how to increase our inner force in order to strengthen our gravity centre and vitality. Nature is a great source of energy and inspiration for me.

I speak fluent French, English and Italian and practice coaching in all 3 languages.
